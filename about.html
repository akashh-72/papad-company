Our Story

Vaishali Sandage Papad Kendra was founded in 1998 by Mrs. Vaishali Sandage with a simple mission - to preserve the authentic taste of traditional Indian papads and sandage while providing employment opportunities to local women.

What started as a small home-based business with just five women has now grown into a thriving enterprise that employs over 50 skilled artisans, most of whom are women from rural backgrounds.

Our founder's dedication to quality and authenticity has been the cornerstone of our success. Every product we make follows time-honored recipes that have been passed down through generations, ensuring that the rich culinary heritage of India is preserved and shared with the world.

Our Mission & Values

What drives us every day

Preserving Tradition

We are committed to preserving traditional recipes and methods of making papads and sandage, ensuring that these culinary treasures are not lost to modernization.

Natural Ingredients

We use only the finest natural ingredients, sourced locally whenever possible, and never add preservatives or artificial flavors to our products.

Empowering Women

We provide sustainable employment opportunities to women from rural communities, helping them achieve financial independence and dignity.

Quality First

We never compromise on quality. Each product undergoes rigorous quality checks to ensure that only the best reaches our customers.

Our Traditional Process

How we craft our authentic products

1

Ingredient Selection

We carefully select the finest ingredients, including premium quality lentils, rice, spices, and herbs from trusted local farmers.

2

Traditional Grinding

The ingredients are ground using traditional methods to achieve the perfect consistency that modern machines cannot replicate.

3

Hand Rolling

Each papad is rolled by hand to ensure the perfect thickness and texture, a skill that our artisans have perfected over years of practice.

4

Sun Drying

Our products are dried naturally under the sun, a process that not only preserves energy but also enhances the flavor and shelf life.

5

Quality Check

Every batch undergoes a thorough quality check before being packaged, ensuring that only perfect products reach our customers.

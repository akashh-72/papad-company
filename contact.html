Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions

How can I place a bulk order?

For bulk orders, please contact us directly via email at orders@vaishalipapads.com or call us at +91 98765 43210. We offer special pricing for bulk orders and can customize packaging as per your requirements.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship our products internationally. Shipping costs and delivery times vary depending on the destination. Please contact us for more information about international shipping options.

What is the shelf life of your products?

Our papads and sandage have a shelf life of 6-8 months when stored in a cool, dry place in an airtight container. We recommend consuming them within 3 months of opening for the best taste and quality.

Are your products suitable for vegans?

Yes, all our products are 100% vegetarian and vegan-friendly. We do not use any animal products or by-products in our manufacturing process.
